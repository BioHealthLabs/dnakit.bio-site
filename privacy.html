Privacy & Cookies

DNAKit.bio is a small, static information site. We do not collect personal identifiers, run accounts or handle payments. Our mission is to provide educational DNA kit information and link to independent comparisons on Genectics.com.

1. Who operates DNAKit.bio?

DNAKit.bio is operated by ronarn (Norway). We maintain a network of educational sites for health, genetics and wellness topics.

2. What data is processed?

When visiting DNAKit.bio, basic technical information may be logged automatically:

  • IP address (anonymised in most dashboards)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and general traffic patterns

This is standard for any website and is used for security, performance and abuse prevention.

3. Hosting & CDN

The site is delivered via static hosting and a content delivery network (Cloudflare). These providers may process technical logs for security and reliability purposes.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics or Google Analytics 4 to understand general usage patterns. This helps us improve content, but we cannot identify individuals through normal use.

5. Cookies

DNAKit.bio sets no login or advertising cookies. If analytics or security tools set cookies, they are solely used for:

  • Anonymous traffic measurement
  • Bot detection and protection

6. External links to Genectics.com

Most outgoing links point to Genectics.com, where detailed DNA kit comparisons, screenshots and affiliate links are hosted. Genectics.com has its own privacy policy.

7. Your rights

If you live in a region with data protection laws (EU/EEA, UK), you may request access or deletion of data associated with web server logs. In practice, this data is extremely limited and often anonymised.
